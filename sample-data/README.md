# Sample Data for Course Exercises

These files are practice data for the pre-course exercises. They simulate the kind of information a PM works with every day. None of this is real company data.

Use these files whenever an exercise says "use the sample data" or when you want something to practice with before using your own work data.

## What's Here

| File | What It Is | Good For |
|------|-----------|----------|
| `user-feedback.csv` | 30 pieces of customer feedback from multiple channels (support tickets, NPS surveys, user interviews, app store reviews, churn surveys) | Feedback synthesis, theme identification, prioritization exercises |
| `web-traffic.csv` | 9 months of web traffic data broken down by source (organic, paid, referral, social) with conversion metrics | Data analysis, trend identification, reporting exercises |
| `meeting-transcript.md` | A product team weekly sync transcript with 5 participants discussing Q2 priorities | Summarization, action item extraction, decision documentation |

## The Fictional Company

All the data comes from a fictional B2B SaaS company that makes project management software. Think of it as a mid-stage competitor to Asana or Monday.com. The company:

- Sells to startups, mid-market, and enterprise customers
- Has ~50 employees and a 5-person product team
- Is growing but seeing mid-market churn increase
- Has a web app and a mobile app (iOS and Android)

You don't need to memorize this. It's just background so the data makes sense when you read it.
